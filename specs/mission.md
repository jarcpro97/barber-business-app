# Mission

CutMetrics exists to turn every independent barber into a confident business owner.

We give barbers a single dashboard to track clients, log daily cuts, understand their income at a glance, and grow their brand — so they can spend less time on spreadsheets and more time behind the chair.

## Goals

- **Financial clarity** — daily, monthly, and yearly income reports that require zero accounting knowledge.
- **Client ownership** — a personal client list the barber controls, not a third-party platform.
- **Brand growth** — tools and insights that help barbers promote themselves on social media and build a loyal following.
